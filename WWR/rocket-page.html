Rocket types and their parts

space capsules

A Lander is a spacecraft that descends towards and then comes to rest on, the surface of a celestial body, usually a planet or moon, and it usually makes a soft landing and is still functional after landing on the celestial bodyâ€™s surface. But not always as there are also impact probes aka impactors which are a type of lander and they are usually destroyed on impact with the celestial body to gain knowledge of the consequences of an impact on said celestial body.

space plane

A Spaceplane is a vehicle that can fly and glide in the Earth's atmosphere like a normal plane and maneuver in space like all other types of spacecraft, there are two types of them low-orbital spaceplanes, which usually tend to be similar to fixed-wing aircraft, and orbital Spaceplanes, which tend to be similar to conventional spacecraft. Both are also usually reusable and stay in service for many years.
